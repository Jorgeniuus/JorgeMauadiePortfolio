Welcome to my Portfolio!

Hello! my name is Jorge Mauadie!

     I am 31 years old and I am a Unity Developer from Brazil with experience of games published on the Google Play Console. I started game development in January 2022 and I always seek to improve my knowledge in the area, being self-taught, studying and learning on my own in order to improve my development and programming skills.

      One of my most notable works is Ship Destroyer, a mobile game in which I developed several skills. I worked a lot on OOP and Design Patterns such as (Singleton Pattern, Object Pooling, Scriptable Object, Observer Pattern). In this game I implemented Google's AdMob advertising system and I also implemented the system of purchases with In-App-Purchase.

      In September 2023 I started learning Front-End with intention of build this portfolio and have my projects combined with my own identity. At the moment I have been learning React.js and Flutter so in the future I intend to start learning game development in VR and AR too.

     I consider myself a person who is passionate about games, from the oldest games to the most current ones. I've been a very creative person since I was a child and I really like the idea of being able to create new interactive worlds and see this interaction between people and the digital world.
