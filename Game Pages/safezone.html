Safezone:

     Safezone is a third-person multiplayer survival game developed for the Steam platform, where you can interact with various players by forming groups and trading with each other in a safe area called Safezone. In this area, you can also interact with NPCs, buying and selling items and accepting missions that you can undertake individually or with a group of up to 4 players.

     In this game, I worked with the Mirror Networking tool, dealing with multiplayer concepts such as RPC, Server Authoritative, and others.
